Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

House of Posh (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or submit a booking or contact form.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our site:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Booking preferences (service, date, time)
  • Messages or notes you send us

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Confirm and manage your appointments
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send service reminders (via WhatsApp or SMS, if provided)
  • Improve our website and services

4. Data Protection

We do not store your data on external servers. Form submissions are sent directly to our business WhatsApp or email. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@houseofposh.co.za.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.

For questions about this policy, please reach out via:
